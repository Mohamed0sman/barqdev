Our Story

BARQ DEV started with a simple vision: make software development accessible to everyone. We noticed that startups and entrepreneurs face significant challenges in turning their ideas into digital products due to high costs and long timelines.

Using the latest AI and automation technologies, we built a platform that reduces development time by 90% and costs by 70%, while maintaining the highest quality standards.
